Our Services

Offering:

Power Yoga, is one of several forms of energetic form of yoga. These include forms derived from Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga and body weight exercises. Is a fast-paced style of yoga that's focused on building strength and endurance. It is an excellent form of yoga for burning calories.

Karma Yoga, defined is the discipline of selfless action, where one does their duty at their best without any involvement of ego or attachment. It is the yoga of action or duty where we learn kindness and compassion without and expectation of gain.

Pilates, is a type of strength training exercise that encourages you to identify and work within the limits of your body. There's also an inherent mindfulness to it and a lot of breath work, which can help relieve tension and stress.

Meditation, is a mindfulness technique to practice focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity - to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.
